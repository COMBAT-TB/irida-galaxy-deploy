COMBAT TB Workbench

The COMBAT-TB Workbench provides easy to use tools for analysing M. tuberculosis genomic sequence samples including lineage typing, drug resistance and the phylogentic relationship between samples.

It was developed at part of the COMBAT-TB project, a SAMRC and DST/NRF Research chair funded project undertaken by researchers an d software developers at SANBI. The COMBAT-TB Workbench is developed using technology from the IRIDA and Galaxy projects and incorporates leading tools developed for M. tuberculosis and microbial bioinformatics.
